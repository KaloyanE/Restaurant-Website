Short History of Our Restaurant

1985

The Happy Chicken Restaurant opened its doors
for the first time in downtown Varna.
The restaurant was founded by a local entrepreneur,
John Smith, who wanted to offer a healthier and
more affordable fast food option to the community.

1990

The Happy Chicken Restaurant had become a popular spot in
the downtown area, and John decided to open a second location in
Varna. The new restaurant was larger and featured a drive-thru
window, which was a first for the Happy Chicken brand.

2000

The Happy Chicken Restaurant had now grown to ten locations
across Varna, and John decided it was time to take the brand national.
He secured a franchise deal with a major fast food corporation,
and the Happy Chicken brand was soon popping up all over the country.

2015

John Smith decided to retire, and the Happy Chicken Restaurant
was sold to a new owner, Sarah Lee. Sarah had a background in finance
and had a vision to modernize the restaurant's operations and branding.

2018

Under Sarah's leadership, the Happy Chicken Restaurant added
several new items to the menu, including grilled chicken sandwiches,
sweet potato fries, and vegetarian options. These new items were a hit with
customers and helped the restaurant stay competitive in a crowded market.

2023

Nowadays The Happy Chicken Restaurant continues to grow and expand.
It has now become one of the most popular restaurant chains
in Bulgaria and is soon looking to go outside the country.
