About Our Ingredients

Our Commitment to Quality

Chicken

We use only 100% white meat chicken in all of our products. Our chicken is sourced from trusted suppliers who meet our strict quality standards. We never use chicken that has been treated with antibiotics or hormones.

Fresh Vegetables

We carefully select our vegetables from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to quality. All our vegetable dishes are freshly prepared and offer a healthy and delicious complement to our chicken.

Bread

Our breading is a unique blend of spices and seasonings that give our chicken its delicious flavor and crispy texture. We use only high-quality ingredients, and we never use artificial colors or preservatives.

Oils

We use 100% vegetable oil to fry our chicken. Our oil is carefully filtered to ensure that it remains fresh and free of impurities. We never reuse our oil, so you can be sure that your chicken is always cooked in fresh oil.

Sauces

Our sauces are made with high-quality ingredients and are carefully crafted to complement the flavor of our chicken. We offer a variety of sauces to suit every taste, including classic barbecue, tangy honey mustard, and spicy buffalo.

Potatoes

Our potatoes are hand-cut daily to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. We use only high-quality, locally sourced potatoes that are carefully selected for their size and texture. Our fries are cooked to golden perfection and are lightly salted to enhance their natural flavor.
